Hello world

A program to greet the planet.

This page is an attempt to greet everyone on earth using our various human languages. we're intrested to see how long our list can become.

we'll start with languages that Acedu staff could offer without using goggle

  • English: hello, world!
  • Yoruba: E n le o, agbaye!
  • Japanese: Konnichiwa, sekai!
  • French: Bonjour, monde!
  • Kinyarwanda: Mwirwe, isi!
  • German: Guten tag, welt!
  • Pig Latin: ello-hay, orld-way!
